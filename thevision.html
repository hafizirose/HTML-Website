The Vision

VISION

Every human being in this world has their own vision and mission.

As for me, I want to live a happy life and make people in my surrounding happy too.

The most significant individuals in our lives would be our family. If our family are unhappy, nothing will make us happy.

Following the completion of my diploma courses, I will pursue a bachelor's degree.

My family would be thrilled to see me graduate with a degree certificate in hand.

The completion of a degree would make many aspects of my life simpler and more manageable.

As a result, if I am successful in finishing my studies, I will get closer to realising my goal.

My subsequent goal is to use the money I have saved up to take my family and parents on vacation.

My parents' health is deteriorating. They will eventually stop working.

As a result, it is up to me to cater to their requirements and whims in order to ensure that they lead a life that is full of contentment and convenience.

When everyone in my family is happy, I am pleased as well.
